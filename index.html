What Is Sustainable Gardening?

Sustainable gardening is a method of growing plants that is mindful of the environment. It focuses on conserving resources, promoting biodiversity, and reducing chemical use.

Why Choose Sustainable Gardening?

  • Reduces environmental impact
  • Improves soil health and water conservation
  • Supports local ecosystems and pollinators
  • Lowers gardening costs over time

Getting Started: Simple Steps

  1. Choose native and drought-tolerant plants.
  2. Use compost instead of chemical fertilizers.
  3. Collect rainwater for irrigation.
  4. Mulch to retain soil moisture and prevent weeds.
